OUR VALUES
RESPONSIBILITY

We guarantee our repairs to be long lasting. If we made a mistake and the original problem comes back or the repaired part breaks down, we commit to fix it again for free.

TRANSPARENCY

Our mission is to repair bikes, not cheating you. That's why we are always honest about your bike's problems and repair options. So no extra charges for made up problems, no expensive repairs of things that are not worth or won't last.

FLEXIBILITY

You study, you work. Your time is valuable. It is our priority to fit in your time schedule so you do not have delays or additional bus costs etc. We are here for you when everyone else is closed. Mobility andavailability round-the-clock make your life easier.

SUSTAINABILITY

Reuse. Recycle. Renovate. If it's possible to save an old bike or its part (tube, brakes, cables, seats, ...), we prefer to repair it and reuse it rather than thoughtlessly replace it for a new one. However, sometimes it's easier and more helpful to install a new part, in that case we forward the metal and rubber waste to be recycled carefully.

ABOUT STAFF
HOW WE DO OUR JOB?

By experience advanced knowledge about local environment, we deliver quality repairs on all bicycle types that are meant to last.
